## The Features That Actually Stand Out

A lot of local SEO tools say they do bulk management. dbaPlatform does it differently in a few specific ways worth calling out:

**Bulk post scheduling without location caps.** Most competitors cap bulk scheduling at around 10 locations. dbaPlatform handles hundreds — you can schedule posts weeks or months ahead across your entire location portfolio in one go.

**Photo management with geotagging.** Photos are automatically tagged with location-specific EXIF data before being published. This is a local SEO signal that most tools skip entirely.

**The industry's first video scheduler for Google Business Profiles.** Videos are increasingly important in GBP, and dbaPlatform got there first.

**60+ directory sync.** Push your NAP data (name, address, phone) to major directories, voice search devices, GPS apps, and data aggregators simultaneously — and suppress duplicate listings automatically.

**Inventory integration.** For vehicle dealers and retailers, dbaPlatform connects product and vehicle inventory directly into GBP listings, enabling free local product listings and vehicle listing ads without the usual feed headaches.

**Local Authority scoring.** This is their proprietary local ranking measurement — a score from 1 to 100 that aggregates brand consistency, listing integrity, reputation, engagement, and influence. It's the kind of reporting that actually shows clients *why* their rankings are where they are, not just what the rankings are.
